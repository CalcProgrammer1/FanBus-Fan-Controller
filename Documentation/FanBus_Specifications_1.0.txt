FanBus 1.0 Protocol Specifications|
----------------------------------|

Introduction

-FanBus is a chainable, serial data bus designed for low-speed communication to case mod and PC cooling accessories such as fan controllers and lights

-FanBus is designed to be simple, cheap, and open

-FanBus is designed to be versatile and supports customized commands for different devices
